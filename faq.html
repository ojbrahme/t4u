FAQs

How is the classes going to function?

We will host meeting and post recorded meeting into the classroom. You may go at your own pace.

How long are classes?

It is dependent on what topic is being covered. If it is a more complex topic, it will take more time. Maybe even multiple lectures.

How can I get help?

Reach out to opbrahme1@gmail.com if you need any help. I will schedule a time for a meeting.

Do I have to do all the work assigned?

No. This is not a graded class. If you want a more rigerous class fill out the form in order to be graded.

Do I have to donate?

No. If you can donate, that would be greatly appreciated. If you want to help out you can volunteer.
